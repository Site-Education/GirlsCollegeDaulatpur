GOVERNMENT GIRLS COLLEGE DAULATPUR


GOVERNMENT GIRLS COLLEGE DAULATPUR HAVE NO OWN BUILDING. CURRENTLY IT IS RUNNING IN VACANT OLD GIRLS HIGH SCHOOL BUILDING WITH EXCEES SHORTAGE OF PROPER LABS AND OTHER INFRA STRUCTURE.

THIS OLD GIRLS HIGH SCHOOL DULATPUR IS HANDED OVER BY EDO SHAHEED BENAZIR ABAD TO GOVERNMENT BOYS DEGREE COLLEGE DAULATPUR IN YEAR 2004

IN 2006 THE GIRLS COLLEGE ESTABLISHED WITH SNE BUT WITHOUT BUILDING WHICH TILL THEN RUNNING IN THAT BUILDING OF OLD GIRLS HIGH SCHOOL DAULATPUR ALONG WITH BOYS COLLEGE DAULATPUR

GOVERNMENT BOYS DEGREE COLLEGE DAULATPUR IS SHIFTED ITS OWN NEW BUILDING IN AUGUST 2023 hence completely in use of Government Girls College Daulatpur.
