IN 2006 THE GIRLS COLLEGE ESTABLISHED WITH SNE BUT WITHOUT BUILDING WHICH TILL THEN RUNNING IN THAT BUILDING OF OLD GIRLS HIGH SCHOOL DAULATPUR ALONG WITH BOYS COLLEGE DAULATPUR
